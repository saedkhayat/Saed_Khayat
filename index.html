Professional Profile

Academic leader and researcher with 20+ years in international grants, water sciences, strategic partnerships, and higher education development.

Prof. Dr. Saed K. Khayat serves as Director of the International Grants and Project Center at An-Najah National University in Nablus, Palestine. He brings expertise in international academic relations, project management, water resources, and education modernization.

His career spans research institutions in Germany, Palestinian universities, and the Ministry of Higher Education. He leads strategic initiatives in internationalization, Bologna Process alignment, water science research, and development of micro-credentials for higher education.

His work connects universities, research institutions, European partners, and international agencies through funded projects, research collaboration, training programs, and institutional capacity building in both water sciences and higher education innovation.
